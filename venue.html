Just 25 minutes from Barcelona and 35 minutes from the El Prat airport, the Campus La Mola is set in the heart of the Sant Llorenç del Munt nature reserve. The site has 185 bedrooms and 22 fully equipped meeting rooms all bathed in daylight with a calming view over the surrounding park. For your celebrations and exclusive events, the Mas Bonvilar, a traditional 17th Century manor house, has been turned into a 450 sq.m. reception hall that can accommodate up to 700 guests.

Camí dels Plans de Can Bonvilar, S/N, 08227 Terrassa, Barcelona, Spain

Phone: +34 936 28 96 20

Fun and entertainment activities

The following are included in the room price:

Outdoor activities: fitness area, mountain biking, football, basketball, handball, badminton, table tennis, outdoor pool, outdoor swimming pool, archery.

Indoor activities: spa, sauna, karaoke, table football, billiards, table tennis, board games, indoor pool, indoor swimming pool.

ACCOMODATION

Accommodation is available in single bedrooms. All rooms are modern, 11sq. m, with a double bed, ensuite bathrooms with separate toilets, wifi and air conditioning. Most of the bedrooms are located in the building where the dining room is.

BOOKING PROCESS

The request for accommodation for F2F participation will be confirmed in EventsCase.

PAYMENT

The accommodation must be paid one month prior to the event. You will be asked to pay directly to the venue by credit card. The venue will email you the payment link with all instructions.

CANCELLATION POLICY VALID FOR ALL PARTICIPANTS (including invitees)

The attendance can be cancelled up to one month prior to the event (by 17 October), by contacting us at biohackathon-europe@elixir-europe.org. Please note, if you cancel after this date or do not show up at the event you will be responsible for full payment, even if you are on the invite list.

COVID-19 MEASURES

We aim to have the exclusive booking for the venue (if enough people book F2F). We are monitoring the situation in Spain and we will inform registrants of changes in COVID restrictions, and how they may effect participants at the Biohackathon.

Taxi

It is easiest to book taxis. The journey is just over half an hour by car from the airport and from Barcelona. Please see the price below for individuals and groups:

  • Aeropuerto / Airport Business Class up to 4 people 95€
  • Aeropuerto / Airport Van Class 7 people 125€
  • Aeropuerto / Airport bus 15 people 165€
  • Aeropuerto / Airport bus 30 people 185€
  • Aeropuerto / Airport bus 60 people 225€

Train

From the airport you can take a train to Barcelona Sants Station (20 minutes) and then change for a train to Terrassa station (50 minutes). From the station you will need to take a taxi for about another 15 minutes.
